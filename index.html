The Edge has been Wichita’s premiere paintball adventure since 1995. The secret to our success hasn’t changed over the years: we offer our customers a safe and exciting environment to play paintball. Whether you just want to come out by yourself for a weekend game or have us host your next corporate outing, The Edge Paintball Adventures will offer one exciting and unforgettable time.

With 7 woodsball courses and 3 speedball courses on our 50+ acre facility, we’re sure to have a little something for everyone. Coupled with our trained and experienced referee staff and stellar pro shop should you find yourself becoming a repeat customer, we’re here to help you every step of the way. So go ahead, give us a call or stop by this weekend to see what we’re all about. The Edge Paintball Adventures – do something different!
